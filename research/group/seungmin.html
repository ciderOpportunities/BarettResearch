Seungmin Lee is a Ph.D. student in Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University. His research interests are in development economics, food & agriculture and labor economics.
Before coming to Cornell, Seungmin received B.S. from the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. He worked as a research assistant at Innovations for Poverty Action (IPA) and Yale University, and as a consultant at Development Impact Evaluation (DIME) at the World Bank.
