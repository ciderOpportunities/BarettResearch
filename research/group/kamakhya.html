Kamakhya Misra

Degree B.A. in Applied Economics and Management Major
Email Address km657@cornell.edu

Kamakhya is a senior at Cornell University, originally from New Delhi, India. She is an Applied Economics and Management Major with concentrations in Finance and Environmental, Energy and Resource Economics. On campus, she is a teaching assistant for Financial Statements Analysis, a research assistant with Cornell Atkinson Center for Sustainability, a financial audit intern with the University Audit Office, treasurer for Forte Campus Cornell, a pre-professional business group for women, and a member of Cornell Anjali, a competitive dance team. Post graduation, she will be working at Rabobank's NYC office in Investment Banking (Mergers and Acquisitions). Outside of classes and professional commitments, she is a classical Kuchipudi dancer with 14 years of training, an avid tennis fan and a holistic living enthusiast.
