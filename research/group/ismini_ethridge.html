Ismini is an undergraduate student in the College of Agriculture and Life Sciences studying Development Sociology. During her undergraduate career she hopes to better understand the theoretical and applied dynamics of economic development and sustainable development in the globalized economy and world. She is also interested in the roles of sustainable business models as well as various aid institutions and how they influence these dynamics.

On campus, Ismini is a member of Cornell's Social Business Consulting club, Cornell University Sustainable Design (CUSD), and works as tour guide for Cornell Visitor Relations.
