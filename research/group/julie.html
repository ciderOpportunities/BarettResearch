Julia Hans

Degree B. S. in Health Care Policy
Email Address jmh529@cornell.edu

Julia is a Junior studying Health Care Policy in the College of Human Ecology. Her interests lie in the intersection of international development and the impact of structural inequality on health outcomes, and how practical interventions can be used to mitigate negative effects. At Cornell, she is an athlete on the Track and Cross Country teams, a member of the Engineering World Health project team, and on the Dean's Undergraduate Advisory Council. In her free time, she enjoys watching documentaries, exploring new places to run, and planning for future travel.
