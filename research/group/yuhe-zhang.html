Yuhe Zhang

Degree M.S. Applied Economics and Management
Email Address yz2978@cornell.edu

Yuhe Zhang is a M.S. student at the Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University. Before joining Cornell, Yuhe received a BSc (Hons) in Economics from the University of Nottingham Ningbo China. Her research interests focus on food economics and applied microeconomics using big data. Her current research seeks to better understand food assistance programs, with an emphasis on the interplay between public and private programs.

In Yuhe’s free time, she enjoys running and playing the violin, and she is a member of the Cornell Chamber Orchestra.
