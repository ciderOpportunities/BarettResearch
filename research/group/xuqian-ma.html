Xuqian Ma is a second-year MS student at Cornell Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management, with a concentration in international and development economics. Her research interests lie in early childhood nutrition, maternal and child health, and gender. Prior to Cornell, she received a B.S. in economics from the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. She worked as a research assistant at Stanford Rural Education Program (REAP) and China Philanthropy Research Institute.
