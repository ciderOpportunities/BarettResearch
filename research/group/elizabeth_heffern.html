Elizabeth is an undergraduate Statistics major in the College of Arts and Sciences. Previously, she has worked as both a student and an intern in Senegal and The Gambia. She also spent time in northeastern India conducting ethnographic research and working on a project to support development of the local tribal population. In addition to her involvement with the Barrett Research Group, Elizabeth teaches for Cornell Outdoor Education, tutors for the Knight Institute Writing Center, and is a Certified Personal Trainer.
