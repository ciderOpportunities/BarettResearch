Robert Hoal

Degree B.S. in Applied Economics and Management
Email Address rgh227@cornell.edu

Robert (Bertie) Hoal is a student in the Charles H. Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management and concentrates in Environmental, Energy, and Resource Economics (EERE). His passions lie at the intersections of environmental and development economics, especially concerning climate resiliency and adaptation. From growing up in the Rocky Mountains to a multitude of international experiences, he has cultivated interests in global environmental challenges. Originally from Golden, Colorado, his free time is spent skiing, hiking, and camping.
