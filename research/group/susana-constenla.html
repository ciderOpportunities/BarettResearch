Susana Constenla is a M.S. student in Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University. Originally from Spain, she got her bachelor’s degree in Agricultural Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Madrid (2016). At the moment she is gathering a more wholistic perspective of the food and agricultural sectors, complementing her technical background with economical training. She is specializing in development economics, with a particular interest in the analysis of food security related issues.
